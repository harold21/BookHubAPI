Delete Your Sabbath School Account

Last updated: April 1, 2026

If you want to request deletion of your Sabbath School account and associated personal data, send a deletion request by email to haroldmax@gmail.com.

How to request deletion

  • Send an email with the subject line: Account Deletion Request.
  • Include the email address associated with your Sabbath School account.
  • Include your full name and, if applicable, the church or class associated with your account.

What will be deleted

  • Your account login and authentication record.
  • Your profile data, including name, email, phone, document number, and related profile fields.
  • Your class membership and role assignments associated with your account.

What may be retained

  • Attendance records and related administrative history may be retained for legitimate organizational record-keeping purposes where required by the church or organization using the app.
  • Minimal technical or legal records may be retained for a limited period if necessary to comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, or enforce our terms.

Retention period

We aim to process account deletion requests within 30 days of verifying the request. Any retained data kept for legal or administrative reasons will be stored only for as long as necessary for those purposes.
